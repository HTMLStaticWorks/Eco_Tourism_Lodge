The Roots of Sylvan

A Vision Born in the Canopy

Sylvan Retreat began not as a business, but as a promise. In 2012, founders Elena and Marc stood amidst this very canopy and realized that true luxury shouldn't come at the cost of nature—it should be nature's reward.

We spent three years mapping the forest floor to ensure not a single ancient tree was disturbed during construction. Today, we stand as a testament that ecological preservation and world-class hospitality can thrive together.

Energy

Solar Energy Grid

Our entire facility operates on a custom-designed photovoltaic array, generating surplus energy that we share with the neighboring community during peak seasons.

Purification

Regenerative Water

We use biological water filtration systems that mimic the rainforest's natural purification process, ensuring that every drop returned to the soil is cleaner than when it arrived.

Humanity

Indigenous Alliance

We are guests on this land. Through our Sylvan Foundation, 10% of every stay goes directly to local medicinal plant preservation and educational programs for indigenous youth.

Protection

The Biodiversity Shield

Our lodge serves as an active wildlife corridor. By limiting our footprint to the canopy level, we allow ground-dwelling jaguars and tapirs to roam freely beneath our guests.
