Born from the Forest

The Vision

A Legacy of Conservation

Sylvan Retreat was founded in 2012 with a single mission: to prove that luxury hospitality and rainforest conservation can coexist. What started as three small cabins has grown into a world-renowned sanctuary.

We believe that the best way to protect our wild spaces is to invite people into them—to let them see, breathe, and hear the heartbeat of the forest.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

100% solar powered, zero single-use plastics, and a closed-loop water system.

Community

90% of our staff are from the local village, and we support local education programs.

Immersion

Every detail of our design is intended to dissolve the barrier between you and nature.
