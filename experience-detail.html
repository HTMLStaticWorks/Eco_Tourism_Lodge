The Emerald Trek

Journey into the Heart

Led by our Chief Naturalist, the Emerald Trek is more than a walk; it's a deep-dive into the complex biology and spiritual significance of the Amazon. You will traverse a 5km trail specifically curated to showcase the transition between primary and secondary forest.

Highlights include visiting a 'Mother Tree'—a 400-year-old Kapok standing 60 meters tall—and discovering clandestine waterfalls where the water is so pure it's drinkable. Expect to see vibrant macaws, playful spider monkeys, and perhaps the elusive giant otter.

Itinerary Overview

08:00 AM

Departure from the main lodge. Introduction to rainforest safety and biodiversity.

10:30 AM

Arrival at the Silver Falls. Refreshments and botanical photography session.

01:00 PM

Traditional wood-fired lunch served in a forest clearing.
